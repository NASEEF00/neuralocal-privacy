Privacy Policy for NeuraLocal

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NeuraLocal (the "App") handles your information. NeuraLocal is developed and published by NSF TECH (referred to as "we", "us", or "our").

We built NeuraLocal as a privacy-first, on-device AI application. Our core principle is simple: your conversations and documents stay on your device. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your chats, and we cannot read your data.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Our Privacy-First Approach

NeuraLocal runs Artificial Intelligence (AI) language models directly on your device. This means:

  • Your chat messages, prompts, and AI-generated responses are processed locally on your phone and are never sent to us or any third party for processing.
  • Documents you add to the Knowledge Base are read and indexed entirely on your device.
  • We do not have user accounts, and we do not require you to sign in or provide personal information to use the App.

2. Information Stored On Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to it, and it is not transmitted to us:

  • Chat history — the conversations you have with the AI characters/agents.
  • Characters / Agents — any custom AI personas you create, including their names, instructions, and settings.
  • Knowledge Base files — PDF documents you upload, the text extracted from them, and the mathematical representations (embeddings) generated for search.
  • App settings and preferences — such as your selected model, theme, and security options.

This data remains on your device until you delete it (for example, by clearing a chat, deleting a document, or uninstalling the App). Uninstalling the App removes this locally stored data.

3. Information Processed Over the Internet

While the AI itself runs offline, certain features require an internet connection. When you use these features, data is sent to the relevant third party as a direct result of your action:

a) Downloading AI Models

The App lets you download AI models from Hugging Face (huggingface.co), a third-party model repository. When you choose to download a model, your device connects directly to Hugging Face. This connection is subject to Hugging Face's own privacy policy.

b) Assistant Tools

The AI can use built-in tools that hand information to other apps or services only when you ask it to:

  • Web Search — opens your device's default web browser and performs a Google search for the query you requested.
  • Draft Email — opens your device's default email app with a pre-filled draft (recipient, subject, body). The App does not send emails itself.
  • Create Calendar Event — opens your device's calendar app with pre-filled event details. The App does not access or read your existing calendar.

In each case, the App simply opens the relevant app/website with the information you provided. The handling of that information is then governed by the privacy policy of that app or service (e.g., your browser, email client, or Google).

4. Advertising

NeuraLocal displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve and measure ads, including:

  • A device advertising identifier
  • Approximate (non-precise) location derived from IP address
  • Device and usage information related to ads

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google may use this information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with its policies.

  • How Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can typically limit ad personalization through your device settings (for example, "Reset advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" in your Android settings).

5. Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions. Each is used only for the feature described:

  • Storage / Files — to let you select and import PDF documents into the Knowledge Base, and to save downloaded AI models.
  • Biometric / Device authentication — if you enable the optional App Lock, your device's biometric or PIN system is used to unlock the App. This is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We do not collect, store, or have access to your fingerprint, face, or PIN data.
  • Internet / Network access — to download AI models and to display ads.
  • Notifications — to inform you of the progress and completion of model downloads.

You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings, though some features may stop working.

6. Data Security

Because your personal content (chats and documents) is stored locally on your device, its security depends primarily on your device's own security measures. We recommend you protect your device with a screen lock and enable the App's optional biometric lock for additional protection.

7. Children's Privacy

NeuraLocal is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App is ad-supported, parental supervision is advised.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Since your data is stored locally and not collected by us, you remain in full control:

  • You can delete individual chats, characters, or documents within the App at any time.
  • You can uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • You can manage ad personalization and advertising identifiers through your device settings.

9. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — advertising — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Hugging Face — AI model downloads — https://huggingface.co/privacy

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be communicated through the App or the app store listing. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
